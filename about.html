Bio

Based in Lisbon, born in London, grown in Madeira. A tropical mess, indeed. I have worked +10 years on Advertising as a strategist and creative on a plethora of industries - from booze to banks, from sports content to music festivals and labels - specializing myself on building business proposals and strategies that usually involved meeting ends between stakeholders and creative panache.

I have a tight link to visuals and the written word since I was but a wee boy. The bits and bobs of stories, the underlying craftsmanship of visuals. Magic and science as legion. In late 2022, I decided to become a UI Designer.

Work aside, I am a father, a gamer, a lover and a fighter. I aim to keep my life on the remote side of the greeny grass. If you think I would be a good fit to your team, please do say hello.
